Average Peer Returns

This data is based on monthly results published by Asset Risk Consultants (ARC). ARC is a peer group benchmark which shows the average returns from discretionary investment managers based on risk profile, after fees.

For the purposes of accurate comparison, we have matched our five strategies to the following ARC classifications:

  • Click & Invest Defensive = Sterling Cautious
  • Click & Invest Cautious = Sterling Cautious
  • Click & Invest Measured = Sterling Balanced
  • Click & Invest Adventurous = Sterling Steady Growth
  • Click & Invest Aggressive = Sterling Equity Risk
